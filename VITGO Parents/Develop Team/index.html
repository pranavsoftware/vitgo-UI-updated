Our Story

VITGO was developed to simplify the transportation needs of VIT University students. Understanding the challenges faced by students in finding reliable and affordable cab services, our dedicated team of developers, guided by a skilled mentor, worked to create a seamless platform. VITGO connects students with trusted cab services while ensuring safety and convenience, making commuting stress-free and efficient.
